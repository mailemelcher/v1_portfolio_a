The Designer

Hello! I'm Maile Melcher,
a multidisciplinary designer and Creative Media student with a passion for crafting meaningful visual stories through brand identity, packaging, and digital design. My work blends thoughtful strategy with creativity, resulting in designs that feel both intentional & alive.

I hold a strong foundation in visual communication and user‐centered design, strengthened by my education in Creative Media and Design and by real-world experience running my own small business, Prana Potions, where I developed every aspect of branding, packaging, and digital presence.

Fluent in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Figma, I also love experimenting with tools like Procreate, Canva, CapCut, and p5.js to explore digital, motion, and interactive art. Whether I'm developing branding concepts, motion graphics, or user-focused layouts, I approach each project with curiosity, precision, and purpose.

Inspired by nature, hiking, and Hawaiʻi's landscapes, I draw on organic balance and flow to guide my creative process – believing that great design should feel natural, cohesive, and intentional. Ultimately, my goal is simple: to create beautiful, meaningful designs that connect people to ideas – bridging creativity and clarity through visual design.

Whether developing a brand identity, refining a digital interface, or shaping a visual story,

my goal is always the same:

to create meaningful designs that connect people to ideas beautifully and intentionally.
