Creating Comfort Lab

Ui | Web Design

From branding to user interface, every element
was crafted to reflect comfort, creativity,
& the beauty of slow living.

Programs Used

The Creating Comfort Lab is a conceptual e-commerce project designed by myself utilizing Photoshop & InDesign.

Inspired by the calm and grounding nature of handmade ceramics, this project explores how thoughtful design can evoke warmth, texture, & calm in a digital space.

With a minimalist layout and soft, earth-toned palette, I aimed to translate the tactile feeling of clay into a visual experience
— merging craftsmanship with modern web design.
